Biography

The protagonist of Fifth Persona is a transferred student at Shujin Academy. At the beginning of the game, he has little power or influence in the world and is restricted by the rules of the system he was born into. However, beneath his quiet demeanor is a strong-willed juvenile delinquent known throughout the world as Ghost. To the residents of the Velvet Room he is known as the Trickster, and to his teammates his code name is the Joker. The protagonist is the leader of the rebellious Ghost Heart Snatchers, who seeks to change society by stealing people's hearts.
